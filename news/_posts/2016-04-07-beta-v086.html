--- layout: kybook_news title: Beta v0.8.6 (build 861) ---

Version 0.8.6 is available for public beta-testing.

What's new

Build 860
  • Slight performance optimization when open a book in EPUB,MOBI,FB2,KB format.
  • Improved support of CSS in the EPUB,MOBI,FB2,KB books.
  • Added an ability to completely remove the margins in the EPUB,MOBI,FB2,KB books.
Build 861
  • Improved selection of text in the books.
  • Added an option for choosing of the interaction method with the content in a book by tapping or long pressing in EPUB,MOBI,FB2,KB.
  • Small changes in the display styles for FB2,KB.
  • Fixed a bug with the inability to bookmark in certain cases in EPUB,MOBI,FB2,KB.
  • Added support for some EPUB3 features.
  • Improved rendering for books with languages written in a Right-To-Left (Arabic, Hebrew).
  • Fixed an issue when view an image from the books at full screen.
  • Added highlighting of a footnote window borders in books with dark themes.
  • Added an ability to apply settings for an individual book.
