--- layout: kybook_news title: Beta v0.8.5 (build 850) ---

Version 0.8.5 (850) is available for public beta-testing.

What's new

  • KyBook is Trialware now!
  • Improved Search (automatic search in a known bad OPDS catalogs was disabled).
  • Improved support for some OPDS catalogs (blah.me).
  • Improved support for Chinese language.
  • Added a message template for the function 'Share' a text from a book.
  • Added more books in the startup package.
  • The Society was temporarily disabled.
