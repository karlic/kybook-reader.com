--- layout: kybook_news title: Beta v0.8.9 (build 890) ---

Version 0.8.9 is available for public beta-testing.

What's new

Build 890
  • Improved support of the metadata in PDF.
  • Changed the rendering of PDF pages.
  • Improved the zooming of PDF pages.
  • Added an ability to choose the zooming mode of PDF pages.
  • Added a feature to choose the display of the added annotations in PDF file.
  • Improved the text selection in PDF.
  • Improved support of speech synthesis in PDF.
  • Fixed an issue with missing or incomplete outline (TOC) in some PDFs.
  • Fixed an issue with navigation to a selected text in PDF page in the some cases.
  • Added an ability to apply settings for an individual PDF.
