--- layout: kybook_news title: Release v0.8.7 ---

Version 0.8.7 is available on the App Store.

What's new

  • Improved support of the metadata in DjVu.
  • Slight performance optimization when open DjVu in scroll mode.
  • Changed the rendering of DjVu pages.
  • Improved the zooming of DjVu pages.
  • Added a progressive rendering of DjVu pages (off by default).
  • Added an ability to choose the zooming mode of DjVu pages.
  • Fixed a rare issue when impossible to bookmark DjVu page in the certain cases.
  • Fixed an issue with navigation to a selected text in DjVu page in the some
  • cases.
  • Redesign of a speech synthesis settings screen.
  • Now changing of a speech synthesis settings will be applied right away.
  • Added an ability to apply settings for an individual DjVu book.
  • Added a setting to display in the footer the number of pages left in a chapter.
