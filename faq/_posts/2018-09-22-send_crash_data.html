How do I send crash data to the developer of KyBook?

Crash data is sent automatically by Apple, if you have enabled sharing analytics.

To enable sharing analytics on iPad/iPhone iOS 11, in the Settings app:
  1. Select Privacy
  2. Enable Share iPad Analytics
  3. Enable Share With App Developers
