--- layout: main_post title: How to select (and edit metadata of) multiple books media: - select_multiple_books_1.png - select_multiple_books_2.png ---

Tap Select menu item, then tap the books you wish to select.

For appearing Context menu with Select item

  • In Card layout - tap the book cover on the right side of the book cell.
  • In Mini/Large Card layout - tap the three dots button.
  • Or use the long press gesture on the book cell.
