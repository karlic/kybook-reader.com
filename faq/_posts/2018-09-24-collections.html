What are Collections in KyBook 3 and how are they used?

Collections are like Tags, but in addition to books, they can also include categories from the Cataloguer (such as subjects, authors, series), folders and even other collections.


Please note, that I am still unsure about the usefulness and future of Collections. The feature is very much experimental and I may remove it in the future.
